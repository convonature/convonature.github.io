South Asian Blossoms

Some of the prettiest flowers regional to south-east asia

Posted by Pragya Rawat on March 20, 2017

China Rose

China Rose is a species of tropical hibiscus, a flowering plant in the Hibisceae tribe of the family Malvaceae, native to East Asia. Photographed in Noida, Uttar Pradesh.

Prickly Waterlily

Prickly Water Lily or Gorgon Plant, is a floating plant with large (4 to 5 feet across), round, deeply veined, flat leaves. The leaves are "quilted" and have thorns on the surface. Photographed in Gangtok, Sikkim.

Banana Shrub

Banana Shrub is a dense, upright, evergreen shrub when young that eventually forms a rounded canopy. It attains a height of 10 to 15 feet in sunny locations. The banana shrub is so called because of the distinctive, sweet banana scent of its flowers. Photographed in Darjeeling, West Bengal.
